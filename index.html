Symmetric 4-node Bennet doubler. Cross-diodes D3 (1→3) and D4 (4→2) provide the regenerative feedback path: when C1 swings to its minimum, |V₁| rises and clamps node 3 through D3, which then delivers charge to C2's swing via Cb. The same loop runs the other way through Ca and D4 on the next half-cycle. The device pumps without parasitic seed (no-swing = z=1 exactly, device = z≈1.20 even with small Cpar), unlike the 3-node basic doubler which needs parasitic symmetry-breaking.
